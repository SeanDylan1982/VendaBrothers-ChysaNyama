A bit about us...

Welcome to Venda Brothers Chisa-Nyama, come and join us for breakfast lunch or dinner! Eat-in and Takeaways available, cash and cards accepted.

All your favourite traditional South African Chisa Nyama classics: Smokey, flame-seered Chicken, beef and wors nyama, coming hot off the braai alongside perfectly prepared pap and the most sensational chakalaka, beans and sauces you've dreamed of and of course tangy coleslaw on the side!

Starting at JUST R35 per plate, NO-ONE leaves hungry!
